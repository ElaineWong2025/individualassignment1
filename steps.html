Steps to Achieve My Dream

  1. Step 1: Research & Choose the Right Course: Like for me, I took Bachelor of New Media Communications (Honours) in University Malaysia Perlis.
  2. Step 2: Check Entry Requirements: STPM/Matriculation/Diploma Holders and MUET is Band 2 and above.
  3. Step 3: Apply to Universities: Submit documents, pay fees, seek scholarships, and potfolio.
  4. Step 4: Build Basic Photography Skills: Practice, use free tools, follow professionals.
  5. Step 5: Use University Resources: Buy a DSLR or mirrorless camera, attend workshops and take internship.
  6. Step 6: Build a Portfolio: Share work online, assist professionals for real-world experience.
  7. Step 7: Graduate & Start Career: Freelance, work with studios, keep learning.

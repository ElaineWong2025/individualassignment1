Steps to Achieve My Dream

  1. Research & Choose the Right Course: Like for me, I took Bachelor of New Media Communications (Honours)
  2. Check Entry Requirements: STPM/Matriculation/Diploma Holders and MUET is Band 2 above
  3. Apply to Universities: Submit documents, pay fees, seek scholarships, potfolio.
  4. Build Basic Photography Skills: Practice, use free tools, follow professionals.
  5. Use University Resources: Buy a DSLR or mirrorless camera, attend workshops and take internship.
  6. Build a Portfolio: Share work online, assist professionals for real-world experience.
  7. Graduate & Start Career: Freelance, work with studios, keep learning.
